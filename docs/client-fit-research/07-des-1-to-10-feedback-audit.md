# Audit des-1 den des-10 theo feedback moi

## Pham vi audit

Audit nay doc truc tiep cac file giao hang `delivery/des-1-home` den `delivery/des-10-home`, tap trung vao `header.php`, `home.php`, `footer.php`, `_header.scss`, `_home.scss`, `_footer.scss`. Khong dung Playwright, khong visual test bang browser automation, nen nhan dinh mobile la rui ro dua tren markup/CSS va cac diem feedback da co.

Feedback moi cua khach gom cac nhom chinh:

- Header bi trung navigation: menu chinh lap lai voi topbar/rail/quick links.
- Naming khong nhat quan: `Blog/Blogs`, `News/Blogs`, `Games/HTML5 Games`, `Score/Review` bi dung lan.
- Footer chua lien quan hoac lap lai: footer co cot/link nghe nhu sitemap chung, khong theo dung concept cua style.
- Mobile risk: search/menu dropdown dung absolute/active chung, nhieu style co rail ngang/topbar/link dai.
- Fake labels/links: nhan nhu `Guides`, `Scores`, `Mobile`, `PC Picks`, `Issue Index`, `News Wall` co nguy co la label trang tri hoac query link khong that su co noi dung rieng.
- Structure qua giong nhau: nhieu mau van la header + hero + blog/review/game sections + footer columns, khac chu yeu o mau va label.

## Ket luan theo feedback

Rui ro lon nhat khong nam o tung mau rieng le ma nam o pattern lap lai: hau het header dang co `main-menu` cong them mot lop shortcut/topbar cung noi dung. Neu khach dang cham "duplicate header nav", can sua theo chuan mot nguon nav truoc khi chinh visual.

Footer cua `des-3`, `des-5`, `des-7`, `des-10` co huong rieng hon; footer cua `des-1`, `des-4`, `des-6`, `des-8`, `des-9` de bi xem la sitemap/link farm hoac lap lai header. Footer nen phuc vu concept cua style, khong chi gom `Explore + Latest + Blog/Review/Game`.

Mobile can duoc xem la rui ro that cho ca 10 style vi phan lon dung dropdown absolute va JS toggle `.active` tu footer. Cac mau co rail ngang/topbar/score strip (`des-1`, `des-4`, `des-6`, `des-8`, `des-9`, `des-10`) can uu tien kiem tra thu cong tu 320px.

De chong "too-similar structure", nen sua theo cap:

1. Header: moi style chi giu 1 primary nav, lop phu neu co phai la latest/topic dynamic that.
2. Footer: moi style co footer signature rieng, khong lap sitemap 3 cot.
3. Naming: chon mot he ten cho tung style va dung nhat quan, tranh `News` neu source la `blog`.
4. Link/label: moi chip/shortcut/CTA phai la link that toi noi dung that; query fake thi bo.
5. Structure: it nhat mot trong cac style gan nhau (`des-3/des-8`, `des-1/des-6`, `des-4/des-9`) can doi thu tu section/footer/header ro hon.
